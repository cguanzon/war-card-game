WAR

  • You and your opponent start with 26 cards each
  • Each round is initiated by pressing the Play! button
  • In each round the top-most card in you and your opponents decks are evaluated
  • The player with the higher card value wins the roung
  • In case of a tie, the next 2 cards are evaluated for each player until a winner emerges
  • The winner of each round will have all the evaluated cards placed at the bottom of his/her deck
  • The game ends when both players get tired/bored. The player with the most cards wins!
